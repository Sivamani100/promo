Account deletion request

Delete your account with clarity and confidence

If you no longer want to use Promo, you can remove your account in-app or submit a request here. Please read the details below first so you understand what will happen and what may be lost.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Promo app and go to Settings > Delete Account for the quickest self-serve option.
  2. If you cannot access the app, send an email request to support@promo.arkio.in with the email address linked to your account.
  3. We will review the request and confirm once the account removal process has been completed.

What happens when your account is deleted

  • Your profile and sign-in access will be permanently removed.
  • Your campaign cards, messages, applications, agreements, saved preferences, and uploaded media may be deleted from active systems.
  • You will lose access to any ongoing conversations, collaborations, saved work, and related account history.
  • Any future activity tied to that account will no longer be available, and you may need to create a new account to use Promo again.
  • Some information may remain in secure backups or logs for a limited time where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal obligations.

Before you submit

Please make sure you are comfortable losing your account data, conversations, and any content connected to your profile. If you are unsure, consider reviewing your account details first or contacting support before proceeding.
