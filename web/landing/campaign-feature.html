Campaign & Agreement

Campaign & Agreements.

Draft structured briefs, execute countersigned legal contracts in-app, track milestones, and manage creators effortlessly.

Brief Construction

Campaign Card Builder

Brands construct clear, standardized brief parameter structures (called Campaign Cards). Define required platforms, hashtag patterns, mention hooks, total budget milestones, and creative references. Our automated layout ensures creators interpret requirements without confusion.

  • Integrated AI creative reference generation
  • Set per-post, milestone, or performance bonuses
  • Cap application capacity threshold parameters

Structured Deliverables

Create and reuse brief cards. Streamline recurring collections or campaign launches without starting from scratch every single time.

Tamper-Proof Ledger

Agreements are cryptographically signed and stored securely. In the event of a dispute, our moderation team references this agreement block.

Contracts

Agreement Builder

Skip the third-party signature tools. Promo automatically creates detailed, binding collaboration contracts when a brand approves an applicant. Both parties sign directly within the app interface.

  • Specify image and video usage timelines
  • Enforce mandatory post retention schedules
  • Countersign with secure cryptographic keys
Execution

Milestone Tracker

Divide larger campaigns into manageable steps. The progress tracker guides both brands and creators through agreement execution, escrow payments, draft review approvals, and publishing schedules.

  • Escrow status indicator checks
  • Direct draft upload for reviewer sign-off
  • Formal dispute logs if deliverables breach terms

Organize Collaborations

Keep all steps aligned. Brands track 50+ creators on a single dashboard, maintaining visibility on exact delivery dates and approval statuses.
