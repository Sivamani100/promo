Before You Leave

Please read these requirements and policies carefully. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed under any circumstances.

1. Settle Balances & Escrow

You must complete or cancel all active campaigns, withdraw remaining wallet funds, and resolve pending escrow milestones before initiating deletion. Accounts with active escrow funds cannot be deleted.

2. Unlink Channels & Metrics

All connected social channels (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) will be permanently unlinked, and historical performance widgets and dashboard archives will be purged.

3. Privacy & GDPR Compliance

Personal profile data is fully erased from our creator marketplace directory instantly. However, tax records, billing invoices, and cryptographic transaction hash logs are retained for statutory legal compliance.

4. 14-Day Safety Hold

Once submitted, your profile is hidden from search metrics immediately. Complete database purge occurs after a 14 business days safety cooling-off period during which you can cancel deletion by contacting support.

Warning: Action is irreversible. All campaign history, rating reviews, and messages will be unrecoverable once the 14-day safety window closes.

Request Submitted

Your account deletion request has been successfully queued. Our privacy compliance group will verify and process the erasure within 14 business days.
