Brand Onboarding

Your first 15 minutes as a Brand

From downloading the app to publishing your first campaign card — follow these 5 steps.

1

Download & Create Your Brand Account

Download Promo from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Open the app and tap "Sign Up". Choose "I'm a Brand" when prompted. Enter your company name, business email, industry category, and create a strong password.

Time Required

3 minutes

What You Need

Business email address

Tip: Use your company domain email (e.g. name@yourcompany.com) rather than a personal Gmail. This speeds up the business verification process.
2

Complete Business Verification

To create and fund campaigns, your brand must be verified. Go to Settings → Verification and upload one of the following business documents:

GST Registration Certificate
Company Incorporation Certificate
Trade Licence
Registered Business PAN Card

The review takes up to 24 hours. You'll receive a push notification when approved. While waiting, you can still explore the platform and draft campaigns.

3

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Promo requires 2FA for all accounts before accessing campaigns or payments. This protects your account and budget from unauthorised access.

1Download Google Authenticator or Authy from your app store
2In Promo, go to Settings → Security → Enable 2FA
3Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4Enter the 6-digit code to confirm — you're protected!
Important: Save a screenshot of the QR code or the manual setup key in a secure location. If you lose your phone, you'll need this to recover your 2FA access.
4

Create Your First Campaign

Tap "Create Campaign" from your dashboard. Fill out the campaign brief:

FieldWhat to Enter
Campaign TitleClear, descriptive name (e.g. "Summer Collection Reels Campaign")
Content TypeInstagram Reel, Story, YouTube Short, X post, or custom
Content GuidelinesKey messages, hashtags, mention requirements, do's and don'ts
Budget / PayoutFixed per-post, milestone-based, or performance-linked
Creator NicheFashion, tech, food, travel, fitness, gaming, lifestyle, etc.
DeadlineApplication deadline and content delivery deadline
Writer's block? Use the built-in AI Assistant to generate a compelling brief from scratch. Just describe your product and goals, and the AI will draft a professional campaign card for you.
✓

You're Live — Now Discover Creators

Once your campaign is published, matching influencers will start applying within hours. While you wait, explore the Influencer Discovery screen to proactively find creators by niche, location, follower tier, and engagement rate. You can also browse the Creator Leaderboard to find top performers.

Creator Onboarding

Your first 15 minutes as a Creator

From downloading the app to applying for your first brand collaboration — follow these 5 steps.

1

Download & Create Your Creator Account

Download Promo from the App Store or Play Store. Open the app and tap "Sign Up". Choose "I'm a Creator / Influencer". Enter your name, email, and choose your primary content niche (fashion, tech, food, travel, fitness, gaming, lifestyle, or other). Set a unique username — this becomes your public Promo Page URL: /@yourusername

Time Required

3 minutes

Cost

100% free

2

Build Your Promo Page (Profile)

Your Promo Page is your digital media kit — it's what brands see when reviewing your applications. Make it count.

Upload a professional, high-quality profile photo
Write a compelling bio that highlights your niche and content style
Link your social accounts (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, X)
Add your follower counts and average engagement rate
Select your content niche tags (up to 5)
Profile completeness matters: Brands are 3x more likely to approve creators with fully completed profiles. Don't leave any field blank — treat this like your professional portfolio.
3

Get Verified & Enable 2FA

Verification is optional but strongly recommended. Verified creators get the blue badge, appear higher in brand searches, and unlock higher-paying campaign tiers.

Identity Verification

  • • Submit a government-issued ID (Aadhaar, passport, or driving licence)
  • • Confirm your linked social handles are genuinely yours
  • • Review takes up to 24 hours

2FA Setup (Required)

  • • Download Google Authenticator or Authy
  • • Go to Settings → Security → Enable 2FA
  • • Scan the QR code and confirm with 6-digit code
4

Discover & Apply to Your First Campaign

Browse the Campaign Discovery feed to find brand collaborations that match your niche. Use filters to narrow by category, payout range, deadline, and location. You can also use the Geo-Map to find brands near your city.

Read the full brief — check the deliverable type, timeline, payout, and brand guidelines before applying.
Write a personalised pitch note — explain why you're the right fit for this specific campaign. Generic notes get ignored.
Tap "Apply" — your full Promo Page is automatically attached. The brand reviews and responds.
✓

You're Ready — Start Building Your Reputation

Once approved, you'll sign a digital agreement, wait for escrow confirmation, create your content, and get paid. Every completed campaign boosts your leaderboard ranking, earns certifications, and increases your visibility to future brands.

Pro Tips for Success

Maximise your results from day one with these expert recommendations.

Write Compelling Briefs

Be specific about what you want. Include content examples, mood references, and clear do's and don'ts. The more detailed your brief, the better the content you'll receive from creators. Use the AI assistant if you need help crafting the perfect brief.

Personalise Every Pitch

Generic pitch notes like "I'd love to collaborate!" rarely get approved. Instead, reference the specific campaign, explain why your audience aligns with the brand's target market, and mention any relevant past experience. Specificity wins.

Always Use Escrow

Never accept requests to pay or get paid outside the platform. Promo's escrow system exists to protect both parties. Off-platform payments bypass all dispute protection and violate community guidelines. If someone asks, report them.

Respond Quickly

Fast response times build trust. Brands: review applications within 48 hours. Creators: respond to approval notifications and messages within 24 hours. Quick communication keeps campaigns on track and builds your platform reputation.

Set Clear Agreements

Before any work begins, ensure the digital agreement covers all specifics: deliverable count, revision rounds, content usage rights, post retention period, and timeline. Clear agreements prevent 95% of disputes. Use the Agreement Builder for this.

Track Your Analytics

Regularly check your analytics dashboard. Brands: track which creators drive the best ROI. Creators: monitor your profile views, link clicks, and application success rate. Data-driven decisions lead to better campaign outcomes over time.
