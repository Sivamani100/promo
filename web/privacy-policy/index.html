Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026

At Promo, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when creating an account, editing your profile, listing campaigns, or communicating with other users. This includes:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password, profile picture, social media handles, and location.
  • Content Data: Campaign titles, descriptions, categories, budget ranges, and chat messages.
  • Usage & Device Data: IP address, device type, operating system, and app interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes including:

  • Providing and maintaining our connection platform between brands and influencers.
  • Enabling user messaging, portfolio showcases, and collaboration agreements.
  • Ensuring safety, preventing fraud, and enforcing our terms.
  • Sending notifications regarding matches, applications, and support tickets.

3. Data Protection and Deletion

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your digital data. You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data at any time. When you trigger deletion in the app (under Settings > Delete Account) or request it online, all your profile details, listings, applications, chat messages, and storage files are permanently erased from our servers within 30 days.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our designated Grievance & Compliance Officer at compliance@promo.arkio.in.
