Advanced Campaign Controls

Promo provides a highly hardened platform designed to protect data, secure milestone agreements, and maintain structural privacy.

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) Enforced

    All profile data, applications, and messaging communications are protected under strict PostgreSQL RLS policies. Only authorized participants have read/write access.

  • Localization Engine

    Specify exact language targets. Influencers who speak and produce content in your target languages can be matched directly, enhancing regional campaign ROI.

  • Full Erasure Policy

    We respect your privacy. Triggering account deletion from Settings immediately deletes all associated profiles, cards, messages, applications, and storage assets from our active servers.
