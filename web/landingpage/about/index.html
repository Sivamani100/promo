Our Vision

About Promo

Our goal is to build the world's most transparent, reliable, and secure influencer marketing marketplace.

Privacy & Integrity

We respect your digital security. Row-Level Security (RLS) guards your assets, and our explicit deletion policy erases all user profile records, media, and chat rooms permanently upon request.

Simplicity Over Noise

We optimize workflows. No long negotiations or disorganized WhatsApp chats. Campaign cards specify clear location, follower, and language needs to facilitate instant matches.

Data Synchronicity

We keep metrics accurate. Influencers can link their social platforms to synchronize real-time follower counts, ensuring brands only collaborate with verified accounts.

Our Mission

Finding the right creators shouldn't feel like a guessing game. Brands spend hours vetting profiles, checking follower quality, and coordinating logistics over DMs, only to receive mismatched results.

Promo was built to solve this. By bringing campaign criteria—such as preferred locations and required languages—directly into public Campaign Cards, we match brands with creators who produce the right content for the right markets.

Backed by our Grievance & Compliance team and secure Supabase cloud backend, we ensure all collaborations are secure, transparent, and built on trusted data.
